Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during a free consultation?

It's a relaxed 20-minute call where we learn about your business, identify the repetitive tasks eating your time, and assess whether AI automation would genuinely help. No pitch deck, no pressure — just an honest conversation.

How much does it cost to get started?

Our quick-win AI setup costs £499 — that includes a business process audit, 2-3 custom automations, integration with your existing tools, and team training. Custom strategy and multi-agent projects are quoted individually after the consultation.

How quickly can Aigenda start working with my business?

We can usually schedule a consultation within a few days. From there, most quick-win automations are live within one to two weeks. We move fast because we know your time is valuable.

Do you work with businesses outside Warrington?

Absolutely. While we're based in Warrington, UK, we work with businesses across the North West and throughout the UK. Most of our work is delivered remotely — location doesn't affect the quality of what we build.
