Who We Are

We're operators who run multi-agent AI systems in production every day. Not academics. Not management consultants. People who build, deploy, and maintain AI tools for real business operations.

We started Aigenda because we saw a gap. Businesses were being sold AI "transformation" projects that took six months and delivered a slide deck. We knew it could be different — practical, fast, and affordable.

So we built a consultancy around what we know works.

Intelligence by Design

Our founding principle: AI should be implemented with purpose, precision, and practical intent — not adopted for its own sake.

Every tool we recommend, every system we build, every piece of advice we give starts with one question: will this actually save you time or money?

If the answer isn't a clear yes, we don't do it.
